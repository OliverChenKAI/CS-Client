What Is Community Service Club

Community Service Clubs are clubs that gives students chance to contribute to the community and gets service hours in return. The Four Community Service clubs in KAS include Red Cross, Food Donation, Charity Art, and Zonta. Each of them is special, they help different parts of the community, so people who needs finanically or other aids will get help.
