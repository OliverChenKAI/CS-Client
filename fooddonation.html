Andrew Charity Association

Andrew Charity Association is created since August of 2011, they help children under 15 and people over 65 when needed by providing resources according to their needs. They have a food bank to keep food and give them to people to prevent starvation. Give familes food and money who needs them. Giveout resources and donate money to the victims of natural disasters, like the 0206 Tainan Earthquake, Typhoon Nepartak, and 0206 Hualian Earthquake.
